# Holiday Money → Pounds 🇹🇷🇪🇺→🇬🇧

A simple, phone-friendly currency converter for holidays. Type a price in Turkish lira or euros and instantly see it in British pounds and pence.

**Use it here:** https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/holiday-money/

## Features

- Converts Turkish lira (₺) or euros (€) to pounds — pick from the dropdown
- Big, clear display showing the result both ways: **£16.10** and "16 pounds, 10 pence"
- Quick-tap buttons for common amounts (₺100, ₺500, €20, €50...)
- Fetches today's live exchange rates automatically when opened
- Works without internet too — falls back to the last known rate, or type in a rate yourself from an exchange office board
- No ads, no tracking, no app store — just a web page

## How to use it on your phone

1. Open the link above in your phone's browser
2. Tap **Share → Add to Home Screen** (iPhone) or **Menu → Add to Home screen** (Android)
3. It now opens from your home screen like a normal app

## About the rates

Live rates come from the free [open.er-api.com](https://www.exchangerate-api.com/) service (mid-market rates, updated daily). Exchange offices and card providers will give slightly different rates, so treat the numbers as a close guide rather than gospel.
